Safety Reminders

If you are unsafe tonight

Call 911 for immediate danger. For shelter, call 211 and ask for “emergency shelter,” “coordinated entry,” “domestic violence shelter,” or “warming/cooling center.”

If someone may monitor your phone

Use a safer device if possible. Domestic violence, trafficking, and runaway-youth hotlines can help with safety planning. Clear browser history only if it is safe to do so.

If a child is involved

Call 911 for immediate danger. For child abuse concerns, call or text Childhelp at 800-422-4453. For online exploitation, use NCMEC CyberTipline.
